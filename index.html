Frequently Asked Questions

What is NAD+ and why is it important?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in all living cells. It's essential for energy production, DNA repair, and cellular communication. As we age, our NAD+ levels decline, which can lead to reduced cellular function and energy.

How does ChronoNAD+ work?

ChronoNAD+ provides your body with the precursors needed to produce NAD+. Our unique formula includes Resveratrol, which enhances NAD+ production and activation of longevity genes called sirtuins.
